This web application helps farmers and researchers identify common diseases in paddy leaves using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model.

The model can recognize the following diseases:

  • Bacterial Leaf Blight
  • Brown Spot
  • Healthy
  • Leaf Blast
  • Leaf Scald
  • Narrow Brown Spot

Simply upload an image of a paddy leaf, and the app will predict the disease with a confidence score and solution.
